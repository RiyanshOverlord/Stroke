About Stroke Prediction

Welcome to our Stroke Prediction system! This application collects a set of health and lifestyle inputs to estimate your risk for stroke. Our goal is to provide you with insights that can help in early detection and preventive care.

Inputs We Collect

Age: Your age is a critical factor, as stroke risk increases with age.

BMI (Body Mass Index): Calculated from your height and weight, BMI helps determine if you're underweight, normal, overweight, or obese. Higher BMI values can indicate increased risk.

Hypertension: Whether you have been diagnosed with high blood pressure. High blood pressure is a major risk factor for stroke.

Heart Disease: If you have any history of heart-related conditions, this will impact your overall risk.

Average Glucose Level: Your blood sugar levels, which are important because high glucose levels can damage blood vessels.

In addition, we collect some lifestyle and demographic details (such as gender, marital status, work status, living environment, and smoking status) which are used to refine the prediction.

What It Means

The system uses a machine learning model (logistic regression) that was trained on a dataset of patient health data. The model combines your inputs to predict the risk of having a stroke. Your prediction score is displayed as a gauge meter on your report page.

A higher score indicates a higher estimated risk. This information is intended to raise awareness and encourage you to consult healthcare professionals if you have concerns about your health.
